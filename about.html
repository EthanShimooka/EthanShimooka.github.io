Works About Resume

Ethan Shimooka is a graduate from the University of California, who spends his time making video games and interactive media. Born in San Francisco, he spent much of his life travelling and absorbing new ideas and perspectives. At the tender age of of 4, he started his gaming career with MegaRace and SkiFree, before moving on to strategy game like Command and Conquer and Total Annihilation. Some of Ethan's favourite hobbies include backgammon, Texas hold'em, backpacking, and cooking.
